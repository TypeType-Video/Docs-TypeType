# Privacy & blocking

TypeType keeps your data on your own instance, and gives you the controls to manage it.

![Manage your stored data](/screenshot-privacy.png)

## Blocking channels

Block a channel and its videos stop showing up across your feeds, home, search,
related videos, and the rest. Manage your blocked channels from settings, and unblock
any of them whenever you want.

## Your data

You stay in control of what is stored:

- **Clear your watch history**.
- **Clear your search history**.
- **Unsubscribe from all channels** at once.

These actions are immediate and cannot be undone, so the app asks you to confirm first.

## What is stored

Your account holds your subscriptions, playlists, history, watch later, favorites, and
settings, nothing more, and all on the instance you (or your admin) run. Nothing is
shared with a third party.
