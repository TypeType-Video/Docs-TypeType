# Finding content

Several ways to discover and reach what you want to watch.

## Search

Search across the active service and filter the results by type, videos, channels,
playlists, and music (songs, videos, artists).

![Search results](/screenshot-search.png)

![Searching from the search bar](/search.gif)

## Home

The home page is your starting feed: recommendations and things to continue watching.
You can trim what shows up, or hide recommendations entirely, in
[Settings](./settings#what-you-see), and choose a different default landing page if you
prefer to open straight on Subscriptions.

![The home feed](/screenshot-home.png)

## Channels

Open a channel to see its videos, playlists, and more, and subscribe from there to add
it to your [library](./library#subscriptions).

## Shorts

Shorts have their own vertical, swipeable surface. Not your thing? Turn it off in
[Settings](./settings#what-you-see).

## Podcasts

Browse podcast content from a dedicated **Podcasts** page.

## Switching service

TypeType supports **YouTube, NicoNico, and BiliBili**. Pick the active service so
search and browsing target the right source, see [Settings](./settings#service).
