# User guide

What you can actually do with TypeType once it is up and you are signed in.

TypeType is a complete, self-hostable **ecosystem** for **YouTube, NicoNico, and
BiliBili**, your own account, library, and player, backed by your own API server,
downloader, and login services, all running on your instance.

![TypeType running on a self-hosted instance](/screenshot-home.png)

## At a glance

- **Watch** from YouTube, NicoNico, and BiliBili in one place, with a real player,
  quality and speed control, cinema mode, native picture-in-picture, subtitles, audio
  tracks, SponsorBlock, and frame-by-frame stepping. See [Watching & the player](./watching).
- **Build a library**, subscriptions, playlists, history, watch later, and favorites,
  that stays on your instance. See [Your library](./library).
- **Find things**, search, channels, Shorts, podcasts, and a home feed. See
  [Finding content](./finding-content).
- **Sign in** with a local account or single sign-on (OIDC). See [Signing in](./accounts).
- **Make it yours**, playback defaults, languages, captions, SponsorBlock, and which
  surfaces you see. See [Settings](./settings).
- **Stay in control**, clear your history, block channels, and manage your data. See
  [Privacy & blocking](./privacy).

## Bringing your data in

Already have subscriptions and playlists elsewhere? You can import them from a Google
Takeout export or a PipePipe backup, see [Importing your data](/self-hosting/importing-data).

## First time here?

If you have not set up an instance yet, start with the
[self-hosting guide](/self-hosting/introduction). This user guide assumes you already
have an instance and an account.
