# Settings

Tune how TypeType behaves for you. Everything here is per-account and saved on your
instance.

![The settings page](/screenshot-settings.png)

## Playback

- **Autoplay next video**, start the next video automatically when one ends.
- **Default quality**, the resolution videos open at.
- **Enable high quality playback**, allow higher-quality streams when available.
- **Compatibility playback mode**, prioritize reliable playback on devices that
  struggle with the default behavior.

## Captions

Customize how subtitles look, size, colors, and background, so captions stay readable
the way you like them.

## Languages

- **Preferred audio track**, the audio language to pick automatically when a video
  has several.
- **Force original language**, keep the original audio instead of a dubbed track.

## SponsorBlock

Decide what happens with community-marked segments:

- **Behavior**, **Skip** automatically, **Mark** them on the bar without skipping, or
  **Off** to ignore SponsorBlock entirely.
- **Categories**, choose which kinds of segment count (sponsor, self-promotion, and
  more), each as Skip / Mark / Hide.
- **Finer control**, show chapters, show the current segment next to the time, mute
  instead of skipping, manual skip for full-video labels, and skip non-music only on
  music videos.

![SponsorBlock categories and controls](/screenshot-sponsorblock.png)

## What you see

Shape your surfaces:

- **Hide in-progress videos** from the home page.
- **Hide personalized recommendations** from the home page.
- **Hide the suggestions column** on watch pages.
- **Hide Shorts** navigation and block the Shorts page.
- **Comments and danmaku**, show or hide them.
- **Default landing page**, open on Home, Subscriptions, or another page.

![Content controls, what shows up where](/screenshot-content-toggles.png)

## Service

Pick the active service, **YouTube, NicoNico, or BiliBili**, so search and browsing
target the right source.
