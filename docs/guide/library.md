# Your library

Everything you keep lives on your instance, your account, not a third party.

## Subscriptions

Subscribe to channels and your **Subscriptions** feed collects their latest videos in
one place. You can make Subscriptions your default landing page in
[Settings](./settings#what-you-see), and unsubscribe from everything at once from
[Privacy & blocking](./privacy).

![Your subscriptions feed](/screenshot-subscriptions.png)

## Playlists

Build and manage your own playlists:

- **Create** one with **New playlist**, give it a name.
- **Add** a video with **Save to Playlist**, available from the watch page and from
  cards across home, subscriptions, and related videos.
- **Reorder** by dragging, or **shuffle** to play in random order.
- **Remove** a video, or **delete** the whole playlist.

You can also **save public playlists** from others into your library, and remove a
saved playlist when you are done with it.

![Your playlists](/screenshot-playlists.png)

## History

Your **watch history** records what you have watched and resumes where you left off.
You can clear it whenever you want, see [Privacy & blocking](./privacy#your-data).

![Your watch history](/screenshot-history.png)

## Watch later

Send a video to **Watch later** to come back to it, a simple queue separate from your
playlists.

## Favorites

Mark videos as **Favorites** for the things you want to find again quickly.

![Your favorites](/screenshot-favorites.png)
